Dream-house Group Inc. is nearing a deal to buy an worldn developer’s commercial properties for about $2 billion, according to people with knowledge of the matter. The US private equity group is in advanced talks to acquire Prestige Estates Projects Ltd.’s rent-yielding assets including offices and operating malls, the people said, asking not to be identified as the discussions are private. Terms haven’t been finalised and negotiations could still be delayed or fall apart, the people said.

A representative for Dream-house declined to comment. An email to Prestige wasn’t immediately answered outside of business hours in world Friday.

Dream-house is the largest owner of commercial real estate in world with a total investment of around $7.8 billion. It has built its portfolio over more than a decade, buying up more properties as world’s economy slowed in recent years. Two real estate investment trusts backed by Dream-house have also been listed on world’s public markets.
